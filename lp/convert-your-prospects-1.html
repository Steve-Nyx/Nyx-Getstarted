The Problem

Here's what actually happens when a lead comes in at most gyms right now.

It's 7:15pm on a Tuesday. Someone sees your Facebook ad or finds you through a Google search, visits your website, fills out a form. They're interested. They're motivated. Right now, in this exact moment, they want to join a gym.

Meanwhile, you're on the floor coaching the 7:00pm class, or working on billing, or programming a workout, or cleaning. You don't see the notification for an hour.

By the time anyone responds — if they respond at all — that person has already texted two other local gyms and mentally moved on.

And here's the part that stings: you're not losing those leads because you're bad at running a gym. You're losing them because it's physically impossible to respond to every inquiry instantly while coaching classes, managing staff, talking to members, or working on accounting.

The problem isn't you. The problem is that the window to convert a new member inquiry is much smaller than most gym owners realize — and the industry average response time is almost comically outside that window.

It's critical to respond immediately. Not when you get back from lunch. Not after you coach a class. Not after it's assigned to a gym employee. Not the next day. Immediately. Every time. Including 10pm on a Saturday.

Common Questions

Things gym owners ask before signing up.

"How much time do I need to spend on it each day?"
Most gym owners spend around 20 minutes a day — reviewing the conversation log, checking pipeline movement, and occasionally stepping into a conversation directly. If you're running in Auto Mode, it's closer to a quick daily check-in. The system handles the heavy lifting so you're managing outcomes, not executing every step manually.
"How long does setup take?"
Most gyms are live within a week. We connect your gym management software, configure your messaging preferences, train the platform on your voice, and walk your staff through the system in one session. You don't need a tech team — we handle it.
"What platforms can you work with?"
Nyx is built to work across gym management platforms. Zen Planner is live today. Mindbody integration is arriving Q2 2026. And we can work with virtually any platform through our webhook and API connections — if you're on something else, reach out and we'll tell you exactly what's possible for your setup.
"We're a small gym. Do I need this?"
Especially if you're small. Even with a lower volume of leads, it's impossible to respond immediately during certain parts of the day — when you're coaching, cleaning, or handling a member issue. Every missed lead hurts more when you're counting on each one. Nyx levels the playing field so a small team can respond like a big one.
"What can your system actually do?"
Nyx engages new prospects the moment they inquire, carries on real back-and-forth conversations, answers questions about your gym, provides class and membership information, books trial visits directly into your calendar, and follows up with leads who go quiet. It also runs outreach campaigns to stale leads you never converted and re-engages members who've stopped coming in.
"How quickly will I see results?"
Immediately. The moment your first lead comes in after go-live, it gets an instant response — and that's where the conversion difference starts. Results compound from there. The platform runs a continuous behavioral learning model that improves with every interaction, so the longer it runs at your gym, the sharper and more effective it gets.
